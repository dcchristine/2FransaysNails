THE FRANSAYS NAILS TEAM

Our Story

“Fransays Nails!” is an online based company that is created on November 27, 2022 with the purpose of creating a bridge between people who want to get their nails done by the available freelance nail technicians within the comfort of their gadgets. Its target market is those who are wishing to avail home service manicure and pedicure services. It also gives a sense of conveniency to the customer because they do not need to go to a specific salon while it creates opportunities for nail technicians to have freelancing incomes.
